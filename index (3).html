Our Story

From McKinney, Texas
to the World

Rohaan Mikaeel Group Inc. was founded with a single mission: to make international vehicle trade as simple, safe, and transparent as buying a car locally.

What began as a small operation serving clients in Pakistan and the Middle East has grown into a globally recognized auto trade firm, exporting thousands of vehicles to over 40 countries across Africa, Asia, the Gulf, and beyond.

Our team of experienced trade specialists, logistics coordinators, and customer advocates work together to ensure every client — whether buying one car or a fleet of machinery — receives the same premium, documented, worry-free service.

Process

How to Buy From Us

International vehicle purchasing simplified into four transparent, secure steps.

01

Choose Your Vehicle

Browse our live inventory online or send us your specific requirements via WhatsApp or email. We source from US auctions (Copart, IAAI, Manheim), franchised dealerships, and private sellers. You can specify make, model, year, color, mileage range, and budget — we'll find the best match.

Browse Online InventoryWhatsApp RequestEmail InquiryCustom Sourcing
02

Confirm Availability & Inspection

Before any payment, we provide you with a full CARFAX vehicle history report, detailed inspection photos, and a written quote. For premium vehicles, we can arrange a third-party professional inspection. You'll know exactly what you're buying — no surprises.

CARFAX ReportPhoto InspectionWritten Quote3rd Party Inspection Available
03

Secure Payment

All payments are made via verified bank wire transfer to our registered US business account. We provide an official invoice on company letterhead, our company registration certificate (EIN), and a payment confirmation receipt. Never send payment to personal accounts — only to Rohaan Mikaeel Group Inc.

Bank Wire TransferOfficial InvoiceCompany Registration ProofPayment Receipt
04

Shipping & Delivery

Once payment is received, we handle all export documentation including the US Title, Export Certificate, and Bill of Lading. We work with established shipping lines to load your vehicle at US ports (Baltimore, Houston, LA) and ship to your destination port. Estimated transit time: 2–8 weeks depending on destination.
